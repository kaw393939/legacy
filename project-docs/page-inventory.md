# Page Inventory

The active Legacy Defenders site profile currently generates 22 HTML pages. This inventory documents the first site built with the framework, not framework-wide required pages.

## Primary User Path

- `index.html`: homepage, situation routing, value proposition, FAQ, and contact form.
- `services.html`: service overview organized by customer situation and scope.
- `pricing.html`: visit, project, and item-handling pricing examples.
- `free-guide.html`: free guide, worksheet, cost categories, and planning prompts.
- `prearranged.html`: plan-ahead page for families trying to reduce future burden.

## Situation Guides

- `single-decision-maker.html`: help for the only decision maker.
- `out-of-state-executor.html`: local coordination for someone managing from away.
- `house-full-of-belongings.html`: inventory, sorting, cleanout, and disposition help.
- `urgent-bills-property-risk.html`: urgent property, bill, insurance, and access risk.
- `professional-referral-partners.html`: attorney, CPA, realtor, fiduciary, and professional partner page.

Situation pages should follow a consistent narrative:

1. Name the situation clearly.
2. Explain what matters first.
3. Show first useful moves.
4. Explain how the active site helps.
5. Show typical costs and what they cover.
6. Clarify boundaries and what the company does not decide.
7. End with a low-friction CTA.

## Education And Trust Pages

- `diy-vs-us.html`: what families can do themselves versus when help is useful.
- `keith-story.html`: founder story and trust-building context.
- `pa-probate.html`: Pennsylvania and Allegheny County probate context.
- `founder-plan.html`: public but noindex founder operating plan.
- `thank-you.html`: noindex post-contact confirmation page.

## Compliance Pages

- `privacy.html`: privacy policy.
- `terms.html`: terms of use.
- `cookie-policy.html`: cookie and analytics policy.
- `accessibility.html`: accessibility statement.
- `service-boundaries.html`: service boundaries and professional disclaimers.
- `refund-cancellation.html`: refund, cancellation, and visit-credit terms.
- `provider-notice.html`: contractor/provider notice.

Compliance pages should stay visible in the footer and included in generated link checks.
