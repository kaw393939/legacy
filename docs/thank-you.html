The next step is usually a free one-hour estate call. If the estate needs someone local to walk the property, we can discuss whether the $250 Estate Math Home Visit makes sense. That visit is credited toward approved Legacy Defenders services for the same property when the project moves forward.

Before the first conversation, it can help to gather:

  • the property address or county
  • who has executor or administrator authority
  • urgent bills, mortgage, utilities, taxes, insurance, or lawn/code concerns
  • photos of the outside and main rooms if available
  • any known cleanout, storage, repair, contractor, or provider quotes
  • any items that may need storage, shipping, sale support, donation, or family delivery
  • the biggest decision you are trying to make right now
