What A First Project Might Look Like

An executor relief sprint might include:

  • a credited $250 home visit
  • room photos and priority item notes
  • a belongings decision session
  • storage, donation, disposal, shipping, or sale-support routing
  • provider calls for cleaning, moving, lawn, maintenance, or repairs
  • receipts, invoices, photos, and next-step records

An example first sprint may cost around $8,500-$12,000. The goal is not to solve every possible issue. The goal is to clear the first bottleneck and give the executor a documented next decision.

What We Do Not Decide For You

Legacy Defenders is not a law firm, CPA firm, lender, realtor, appraiser, insurance agency, or licensed trade contractor. We help organize the house-side work so the executor and appropriate professionals have clearer information.
