Pause first

Do not let urgency push you into the cheapest-looking option.

  • Do not accept a cash offer just because the house feels impossible.
  • Do not pay for a cleanout before documents, valuables, photos, and sentimental items are protected.
  • Do not start repairs before you understand authority, cash pressure, and the likely outcome.
  • Do not let strangers turn a family home into a bargain-hunter estate sale if that does not feel right.
01

Secure the house

Locks, keys, leaks, heat, water, electric, mail, lawn, insurance, and signs of damage come first.

02

Photograph before moving

Room photos protect memories, family decisions, insurance questions, repairs, and estate records.

03

Find bills and authority

Mortgage, taxes, utilities, will, letters, insurance, and attorney questions shape what can happen next.

04

Get one calm plan

A triage call helps decide what is urgent, what can wait, and what professional should review the next step.
