## What Legacy Defenders Does

Legacy Defenders helps families, executors, heirs, and referral partners organize the practical house-side work around inherited homes. That can include planning visits, belongings workflows, item routing, provider coordination, photos, receipts, scopes, records, and project reporting.

## What Legacy Defenders Does Not Do

Legacy Defenders is not a law firm, CPA firm, tax preparer, lender, insurance agency, appraiser, auctioneer, real estate brokerage, home inspector, or licensed trade contractor. We do not decide legal ownership, probate authority, tax treatment, financing approval, insurance coverage, appraised value, listing strategy, or licensed construction requirements.

## Professional Review

Legal, tax, lending, insurance, appraisal, real estate, licensed trade, donation, estate distribution, and custody questions should be reviewed by the appropriate qualified professional. We can help organize information and questions for those professionals, but we do not replace them.

## Executor Control

The executor, administrator, authorized heir, homeowner, or other legally authorized person must approve access, spending, belongings movement, provider work, and final decisions. We help make the work clearer; we do not take control of the estate.
