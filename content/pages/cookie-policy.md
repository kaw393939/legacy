## Cookie Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026.

Cookies and similar browser storage help websites remember choices and understand use. Legacy Defenders keeps this simple: essential storage is used for basic site operation and cookie preferences, and analytics is disabled unless accepted.

## Essential Storage

Essential storage may remember your cookie preference, support accessibility or navigation behavior, and help the site function. Essential storage cannot be disabled through the cookie banner because it is needed for the preference system and basic site operation.

## Analytics

Analytics helps us understand which pages, buttons, and topics are useful. Analytics is disabled unless you accept analytics cookies through the consent banner or preferences panel.

## Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies are not currently used. If marketing or remarketing tools are added later, they should stay disabled unless clearly disclosed and accepted.
