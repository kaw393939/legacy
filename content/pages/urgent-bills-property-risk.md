## The estate can lose money while everyone is still deciding

Some inherited-home problems do not wait for probate, family meetings, or a sale decision. Heat can fail. Pipes can leak. Grass can overgrow. Insurance can change. Utilities can shut off. A small maintenance issue can become a major estate expense.

This path is for families who need to stabilize the property, understand the monthly pressure, and make the next decision with fewer surprises.
