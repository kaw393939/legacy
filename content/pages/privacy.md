## Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026.

Legacy Defenders collects information you choose to share so we can respond to inquiries, understand an inherited-home situation, schedule calls or visits, prepare service scopes, and keep basic business records.

## Information We May Collect

- contact details such as name, email, phone number, and home location
- information about the home, belongings, urgency, authority, service needs, and family situation that you choose to provide
- messages, call notes, visit notes, photos, documents, receipts, scopes, or project records if you later work with us
- technical information such as browser, device, pages viewed, referral source, and approximate location when analytics is enabled

## How We Use Information

We use information to respond to you, provide requested services, prepare practical plans, coordinate approved work, keep records, improve the website, protect against misuse, and meet legal or business obligations.

## Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers or professionals when needed to respond to your request, coordinate approved work, operate the website, process forms, keep records, or comply with law. Examples may include email/form tools, storage providers, contractors, cleaners, movers, attorneys, CPAs, lenders, insurers, appraisers, realtors, licensed trades, or other providers you approve or ask us to coordinate.

## Cookies And Analytics

Essential site storage may be used to remember cookie preferences and support basic site functions. Analytics is disabled unless accepted through the cookie controls. You can reopen Cookie Preferences from the footer.

## Retention

We keep inquiry records only as long as reasonably needed to respond, provide requested services, keep business records, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations.

## Security

No website or email system can promise perfect security. We use reasonable care, limit access where appropriate, and ask that you avoid sending highly sensitive personal, legal, financial, medical, or account information through the public contact form unless specifically requested through a secure process.

## Children

This website is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13.

## Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact Legacy Defenders at care@legacydefenders.com or (973) 637-7079.
