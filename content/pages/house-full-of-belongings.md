## The house cannot move forward while every room is undecided

Belongings are emotional, practical, and financial at the same time. Some items are memories. Some are paperwork. Some may have value. Some are simply blocking the cleanout, repairs, photos, appraisal, listing, or move-in decision.

The goal is not to throw everything away faster. The goal is to make the right decisions once, document them, and clear the house without losing control of the family story.
