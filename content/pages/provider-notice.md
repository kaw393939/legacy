## Provider And Contractor Notice

Legacy Defenders may coordinate independent providers, contractors, movers, cleaners, storage providers, licensed trades, real estate professionals, attorneys, CPAs, lenders, insurers, appraisers, or other specialists. Those providers remain responsible for their own licensing, insurance, contracts, pricing, work quality, and legal obligations.

## Pennsylvania Home Improvement And Licensed Work

Pennsylvania home improvement and licensed trade work may require registration, licensing, insurance, written contracts, permits, inspections, or other requirements. Clients should review provider credentials and agreements before authorizing work.

## No Automatic Endorsement

A provider referral, coordination role, preferred rate, or repeated relationship does not guarantee outcome, availability, lowest price, licensing status, insurance coverage, or suitability for every project.

## Client Approval

Clients or authorized estate representatives should approve scopes, spending, access, and provider work before it begins. Legacy Defenders can help organize scopes, photos, receipts, invoices, and updates so decisions are easier to review.
