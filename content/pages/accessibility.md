## Accessibility Statement

Legacy Defenders aims to make this website accessible, readable, and usable for people using different devices, assistive technologies, and browsing settings.

The site includes responsive layouts, semantic headings, skip links, keyboard-accessible navigation, descriptive link text where practical, and alt text for meaningful images.

## Need Help?

If you have trouble using the website or need information in another format, contact care@legacydefenders.com or (973) 637-7079. Please describe the page, the issue, and the format or support that would help.

We will make reasonable efforts to provide the information another way.
